# Cert-Expert — Website Design Brief (for FOAOS / Tarak)

**Date:** 2026-06-07 · **From:** Marwan Mahra · **Scope:** First public website (cert-expert.de) — brand-led redesign.

---

## 1. Goal
Set the **design foundations** for Cert-Expert's public presence — cornerstones that cascade to everything else (decks, documents, social, future product UI). This first site is about **representation and trust**. We want depth and conceptual proposals, not a logo refresh.

## 2. Who we are — and what's actually behind it
Cert-Expert makes security companies **certification-ready** (DIN 77200-1/-2, ISO 9001/14001/45001) — *"from the security industry, for the security industry."*

But the important part for design: **the brand is not a claim, it's how we operate.** The mantis philosophy (precision, systems, calm, execution) is literally built into the business:
- **Done-for-you, low effort:** the client gives only **simple inputs** (a few forms); **we produce the heavy, audit-ready documentation.** Certification-ready in ~6 weeks, minimal effort for their team.
- **We run like a system:** a production engine that generates the documentation, a **transparent, staged client journey** (the client always knows exactly where they stand), and an **auto-assembled, audit-ready folder** handed to the certifier (DEKRA).
- **Specialist depth + network:** deep DIN 77200 / security-industry expertise; established **DEKRA** relationship and a network of auditors.
- **Real traction:** active security clients in certification (e.g. TeamFlex, SecuGuard, Schutzritter, Wolf Street, ZT, ELC), audits scheduled now.

> **The congruence:** when a visitor lands on the site, it should *feel like the inside of a precise, calm system* — because that is what Cert-Expert is. Substance first.

**Trajectory (outlook, not the headline):** we are building **Certification OS** — the platform where a security company becomes *and stays* certified with minimal effort: **self-serve + done-for-you** (pick your standards, pick your certifier, give simple inputs → we produce everything), with targeted advisory calls as the premium touch, and recurring **care + employee training** built in. Think *Check24 meets Vanta* for DIN/ISO in physical security, on a monthly subscription. Design should leave **room to grow into this** (a system, not a brochure) — but the live message stays today's reality; don't sell the platform yet (in build).

## 3. Audience
Owners / managing directors of **security service companies**, small–mid sized, who need certification to win contracts and tenders. They value reliability, clarity, and someone who **takes the burden off them**. German market first.

## 4. What the site must achieve
- Signal **specialist authority** in the security industry — not "another QM consultancy."
- Make the core promise instantly clear: **you give us little, we make you certification-ready.**
- Build **trust at first glance** — premium, calm, credible (proof: clients, DEKRA, the man behind it).
- Carry the brand **soul** into how it *feels*, not just what it says.

## 5. Brand essence — the Mantis philosophy (the soul)
Strength through **precision, patience, observation, intelligence, exact execution** — never noise or dominance. Five principles:
1. **Intelligence** — decisions from facts and experience; causes, not symptoms.
2. **Precision** — every element has a purpose; no unnecessary complexity.
3. **Calm & control** — order through systems; chaos is the absence of structure.
4. **Excellent execution** — ideas are worthless without delivery.
5. **Quiet authority** — competence needs no self-display; trust comes from results.

> Feeling: **authority through substance, not volume.**

## 6. Differentiators to make visible (so design isn't generic)
The site should *show*, not just claim, these — they're our real edge:
1. **Low-effort certification** — "simple inputs from you → audit-ready documentation from us." (Possibly a simple visual: client gives a little → we produce a lot.)
2. **Transparency / you always know where you stand** — the staged journey (a calm "progress" idea, like knowing each step is handled).
3. **Specialist, not generalist** — built only for the security industry.
4. **Proof** — real clients + DEKRA + the founder's industry background.

## 7. Look & feel direction
- **Calm, premium, reduced, precise.** Generous whitespace, strong hierarchy, nothing busy.
- **Dark / elegant** leaning (fits "quiet authority") — confirm via color worlds below.
- **No stock photos.** Mantis-led imagery, clean and intentional. The visual system itself should embody precision (detail, geometry, restraint).
- Typography that feels exact and confident.
- **Anti-references:** generic WordPress/consultancy templates, iStock, loud sales language, clutter — i.e. the opposite of the current site.

## 8. Design cornerstones to lock (our session)
1. **Color world** — lead direction from FOAOS palettes (B&W · Vermilion · Electric Cobalt · Premium Violet · Modern Indigo). Recommendation: a deep, calm one for "quiet authority."
2. **Typography system** (display + body).
3. **Imagery principle** — Mantis-led, no stock.
4. **Layout language** — grid, spacing, how a section breathes.
5. **Hero concept** — one strong headline + Mantis visual.

## 9. Pages & content
Home · DIN 77200 · ISO 9001 · ISO 14001 · ISO 45001 · About · Contact.
Trust elements: client/partner logos (TeamFlex, SecuGuard, DEKRA, Bellator), Marwan as the face.

## 10. Messaging / claims (brand-true — antithesis style)
- "Precision over haste. Systems over chaos. Impact over self-display."
- "Observe. Understand. Structure. Execute. Improve."
- "Order where there is complexity. Certainty where there is doubt."
- "From the security industry — for the security industry."
- Core promise: **"You give us simple inputs. We make you certification-ready — fast, secure, structured. And keep you that way."**
> *[Marwan to confirm the single primary tagline.]*

## 11. Explicitly out of scope (now)
- The internal tool / future customer portal (separate product, login-gated, own design later). The website must **not** depend on or oversell it.
- Full multi-page copywriting, blog/e-commerce.

## 12. What we hope from you (Tarak)
Go deep: turn the mantis idea into a **coherent visual system with soul** (detail, motion, restraint) — not a template. Bring options, challenge our assumptions. You were chosen for exactly this depth.
